10 Best Las Vegas Hotels for a Romantic Couples Getaway

March 28, 2026

Las Vegas is far more than bright lights and bustling casinos. For couples seeking romance, the city offers some of the most luxurious hotel experiences in the world. From stunning suites with panoramic Strip views to world-class dining and serene pool retreats, Las Vegas knows how to set the stage for an unforgettable getaway. Here are ten exceptional hotels that deliver the perfect blend of luxury, intimacy, and excitement for couples.

1. Bellagio

The Bellagio remains the gold standard for romantic elegance on the Las Vegas Strip. Its iconic fountains provide a mesmerizing backdrop for evening strolls, and the conservatory and botanical gardens offer a peaceful escape from the energy of the casino floor. Couples will appreciate the refined suites, the acclaimed restaurants, and the overall atmosphere of timeless sophistication. Request a fountain-view room to enjoy the water shows from the comfort of your suite.

2. Wynn Las Vegas

The Wynn consistently ranks among the finest luxury hotels in the world, and it is easy to see why. Every detail has been considered, from the plush bedding to the floor-to-ceiling windows that frame stunning views of the mountains or the resort's lush golf course. Couples will love the intimate dining options, the upscale shopping esplanade, and the serene pool area surrounded by mature landscaping that feels worlds away from the Strip.

3. The Venetian

With its Grand Canal, gondola rides, and Italian-inspired architecture, The Venetian transports couples to the romance of Venice without leaving Nevada. Every room is a suite, meaning you get generous living space along with luxurious appointments. The resort's restaurant collection features celebrated chefs, and the pool deck on the terrace level provides a tranquil retreat. A gondola ride together through the indoor canal is a quintessential Vegas romantic moment.

4. ARIA Resort & Casino

ARIA brings modern luxury to the center of the Strip within the CityCenter complex. The rooms feature advanced technology, sleek design, and expansive windows with spectacular views. Couples seeking culinary excellence will find exceptional options here, and the three-level pool complex offers a mix of energy and seclusion. The hotel's contemporary art collection adds a cultural dimension that pairs beautifully with a romantic weekend.

5. The Cosmopolitan

The Cosmopolitan is the hotel of choice for couples who want luxury with a creative edge. Its terrace suites feature private balconies overlooking the Bellagio fountains, a rarity on the Strip. The hotel's nightlife, rooftop pool, and eclectic restaurant lineup attract a sophisticated crowd, and the art installations throughout the property give it a personality that is distinctly its own. It is the ideal pick for couples who want their Vegas trip to feel fresh and vibrant.

6. Encore at Wynn

Encore, the sister property to the Wynn, matches its sibling in quality while offering its own distinct character. The suites here are among the most spacious on the Strip, and the European-style pool area, complete with poolside gaming and cabanas, is perfect for couples who want to lounge in style. The resort also features outstanding dining and a more intimate atmosphere than many of the larger mega-resorts, making it ideal for a quiet romantic retreat.

7. Vdara Hotel & Spa

Vdara is a hidden gem within the CityCenter complex, offering an all-suite, non-gaming, non-smoking experience that prioritizes tranquility. For couples who prefer to escape the constant hum of slot machines and casino floors, Vdara delivers a peaceful sanctuary with spacious suites, a rooftop pool, and a full-service spa. Its central location means the excitement of the Strip is just steps away when you want it, but the hotel itself feels like a serene urban retreat.

8. The Palazzo at The Venetian

Connected to The Venetian but with its own refined identity, The Palazzo elevates the suite-only concept with even larger rooms and a sophisticated atmosphere. The sunken living rooms, luxurious bathrooms, and premium bedding create a space that feels genuinely residential. Couples will enjoy the curated dining options and the resort's emphasis on personalized service. It strikes the perfect balance between grand resort amenities and intimate boutique-hotel attention.

9. Waldorf Astoria Las Vegas

Formerly known as the Mandarin Oriental, the Waldorf Astoria Las Vegas stands as a non-gaming haven of refined luxury. Occupying floors in a gleaming tower at CityCenter, the hotel offers dramatic skyline views, an award-winning spa, and understated elegance in every detail. Couples will appreciate the sophisticated atmosphere, afternoon tea service, and the overall feeling that this property exists on a different plane from the bustling Strip below. It is Vegas luxury at its most polished and serene.

10. Four Seasons Hotel Las Vegas

Occupying the top floors of the Mandalay Bay tower, the Four Seasons operates as a completely separate hotel with its own entrance, lobby, and pool. The result is a boutique-like experience within a major resort complex. Known for impeccable service, the Four Seasons provides couples with a sanctuary of calm while still offering access to the entertainment and dining of Mandalay Bay. The private pool area and the hotel's legendary attention to detail make it a standout choice for a romantic stay.
